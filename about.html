    • About Classroom Insights AI

    Classroom Insights AI is simply a tool that we created to collaborate with educators to help them create a safer and secure environment for our children while enhancing and automating administrative tasks such as marking attendance register or number of students in a classroom.

    Classroom Insights AI is not a tool that we created to replace teachers or make them feel unappreciated. Artificial Intelligence cannot posses parenthood instincts like teachers can for example.The tool cannot step in quickly to stop fights physically if they were to break out.

    We created this tool to tackle & monitor issues such as bullying, harassment , abuse and violence in our classrooms.Additionally it facilitates learner observation for classroom engagement, interest, and academic progress, creating a safer and more efficient learning environment.

    Classroom Insights AI Functionalities




    CLASS ATTANDANCE REGISTER

    the application helps in identifying which students are active/present and which students are absent during classes everyday.

    LEARNER OBSERVATION & ENGAGEMENT

    the application uses its advanced AI facial recognition & pose estimation features to observe learner beheviour and how they are engaging with teachers & other learners during classes while respecting data privacy

    CLASSROOM OBSERVATION

    Facial expressions and body language are analyzed locally to gauge student engagement and interest levels during lessons. Teachers can gain valuable insights into classroom dynamics and teaching effectiveness

    INDIVUDUAL PROGRESS MONITORING

    Tracking individual student participation and interaction with the smartboard enables personalized feedback and recommendations.

    MONITORING SEXUAL HARRASMENT & MISCONDUCT

    Continuous monitoring of the classroom environment helps detect inappropriate behavior, enabling prompt interventions.

    VIOLENCE MONITORING

    the application allows easy monitoring of violence in class rooms during school hours using built in cameras on the smartboard

    ENHANCING SAFETY & SECURITY

    utilize facial recognition technology, object identification, and pose estimation to create a secure and controlled atmosphere within classroom

    CURBING PHYSICAL ALTERCATIONS

    The object identification capability quickly detects objects commonly used in fights, ensuring immediate alerts to teachers or administrators.
